Program Overview

Save on Energy Upgrades with CleanBC Rebates

The CleanBC Better Homes initiative offers rebates on heat pumps, windows, insulation, and more. Two programs available — one income-qualified (ESP) and one open to all B.C. households (HRR).

ProNexus Construction works with a registered program contractor. We handle all the paperwork so you save without the hassle. Fill out the intake form below and we'll tell you what you qualify for.

Available Rebates

What Upgrades Qualify?

Rebates available for a range of energy-efficient home upgrades. Amounts vary by program and income level.

✓ Heat Pumps (whole home & mini-split)
✓ Windows & Doors
✓ Attic, Wall & Basement Insulation
✓ Heat Pump Water Heaters
✓ Electrical Service Upgrades
✓ Ventilation (HRV/ERV)

Maximum rebates range from $800–$19,000 depending on the upgrade and program. Bonus rebates available when you complete 2+ upgrades.
All rebate amounts are maximums and are subject to government approval and funding availability.

Eligibility

Is Your Home Eligible?

Most B.C. homeowners with an existing home qualify. Not sure? Fill out the intake form and we'll check for you.

✓

Primary residence in B.C., at least 12 months old

✓

Single-family, duplex, triplex, townhome, or mobile home

✓

Account with BC Hydro, FortisBC, or participating utility

✓

Installed by a registered contractor

✗

Condos, apartments, new construction (under 12 months), garages & outbuildings not eligible

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about the CleanBC Better Homes rebate programs.

Do I apply for the rebate myself? ▼
We work with a registered contractor who handles the application. We coordinate everything so you save upfront, no waiting.
How long does it take? ▼
Eligibility takes a few days. Installation is typically within 2–4 weeks. The rebate is deducted from your invoice upfront, so you save immediately.
Can I combine multiple upgrades? ▼
Yes — completing 2+ upgrades unlocks bonus rebates up to $2,000. We'll help you plan the best combination.
What if I rent my home? ▼
Renters may be eligible with landlord consent and a utility account in the resident's name.
Is there a deadline? ▼
Eligibility codes are valid for 6 months. We'll make sure everything is completed in time.
Not sure what I qualify for? ▼
Fill out our intake form and we'll review your eligibility and walk you through every step.
