Our Story

Over a Decade of Okanagan Craftsmanship

ProNexus Construction was founded in Kelowna with a simple mission: build homes that stand the test of time. What started as a two-person team has grown into one of the Okanagan's most respected residential construction companies.

We've had the privilege of shaping Kelowna's residential landscape — from custom lakefront estates in the Mission to family homes in Glenmore and renovations throughout West Kelowna and Lake Country.

Every project, whether a full custom build or a kitchen refresh, receives the same commitment to quality, transparency, and craftsmanship that has defined us from day one.
